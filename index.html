Website Redesign

Illustrating the redesign process using the Brown CS Pathway's website

This page walks you through the steps of redesigning a website using the course pathways page from Brown University's Computer Science Department as an example. I chose this website because it's one I interact with often and often find it takes me a while to get the information I need.


After self-assesing I also ran the website through WebAIM WAIVE an online tool that scans and detects accessibility problems of a given website. Unsurprisingly WebAIM didnâ€™t find many accessibility concerns: the page is (besides the navigation bar and footer) entirely text based.

This strength of the previous iteration of the website is something to keep in mind while redesigning. The aim is to address the memorability and efficiency concerns without compromising the website's accessibility.
